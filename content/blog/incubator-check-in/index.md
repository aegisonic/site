Aegisonic is officially halfway through Mount Sinai’s DIH Incubator.

So far, we’ve been heads down optimizing our algorithm, miniaturizing our device, and have completed our initial portable prototype! We also welcomed two masters student interns from CUNY (City College of New York) to our team who’ve provided support for research and development. By the end of the incubator, we aim to have our chipset fully installed within the headset, ready for early user testing!

We will be participating in our demo day June 2nd at the Discovery & Innovation Hub at Mount Sinai in Harlem, NY. Hope to see you there!
